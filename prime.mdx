## Trading Strategy
We earn only when you earn. Payment is only made at the end of the period. Our strategy involves reducing risk to the level of the stock market while achieving greater efficiency through greater volatility. The average return for the quarter is 10.09%.

## Security
Money does not leave you! When you create an account on a popular exchange, you connect via secure API technology, and you can disconnect your access keys from the TSFC system at any time. We cannot withdraw or deposit your funds. Investors have 24/7 access to a personal account where they can track information on the trust management agreement. Every quarter we send a report on the results of funds management.
