## Security and Control

All client funds remain in their exchange accounts and the API keys used by TSFC do not allow access to withdrawals. This ensures capital protection, eliminating the risk of fraud. 

## Success Fee Model

We only charge a fee on profits. Clients pay 30% of the income quarterly if the investment is successful. This incentivizes us to maximize results for each user. 

## Minimal Deposits

TSFC is available even for users with small deposits, giving everyone the opportunity to start their crypto-investing journey without the need for large investments. You can connect a exchange account, even with just $100.

## Transparency and Trust

We avoid hidden fees and maintain full transparency of all transactions. Unlike other platforms, our commission is charged only per result, not per transaction.

## Automated Management

TSFC removes the need for users to monitor the market and analyze signals - our professional analysts do it for you.
